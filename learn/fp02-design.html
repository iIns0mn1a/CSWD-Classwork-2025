Skating Website - Gilroy McCalla Jr.

My idea for this website is to make pages about the basics behind the different types of skating.

  • index.html

    This will be the main page to introduce the user to the page concept

  • ice-skating.html

    This site will explain the basics of ice skating at recreational level.

    • ice-hockey.html

      This page will explain the basics of ice hockey skating techniques. (punch turns, crossovers, ect.)

  • roller-skating.html

    This page will give basics on roller skating and how it differs from ice skating (from my personal experience).

  • longboarding.html

    This page will give basic introduction to longboarding. Giving basics of how to skateboard and what can be done with a longboard specifically.

  • free-skating.html

    This page will introduce the concept of free-skating and it's benefits such as it's small form factor.

  • contact.php

    Users can fill out a form to make contact with me. The form will also ask people their current knowledge on skating, and which types they're interested in.
