Skating

Whether you're gliding across ice, rolling down the street, or carving up the pavement on a skateboard, skating is all about freedom, movement, and fun! In this website, you'll be introduced to different types of skating you might be interested in!

Below will be small intros to the different types of skating that will be expanded on in this website!

Ice Skating

The feeling of gliding across the ice could never be topped. The freedom you feel within that rink is euphoric. Let me tell you a bit about ice skating.

Ice Hockey

Fast and aggressive. These two adjective sit at the very heart of ice hockey. From slapshots to slapfights, Come find out here!

Roller Skating

Explore the world on 4 wheels! The best joy of roller skating is being able to explore anywhere. The roller skating webpage will tell you all you need to know.

Longboarding

From cozy cruises around the world, to barreling down hills at 45 mph. Longboarding has grown to be a very entertaining sport, separate from its parent; skateboarding. Learn some of the basics on the longboarding page.

Freeskating

A new "free" twist on roller skating. By combining both roller skating and skateboarding, freeskating was born for an even more simple way of traveling. Check it out on the Freeskating page!
