Ice Hockey


Advanced Skating Techniques

  • Tight Turns (Punch Turns)
    • Used for quick changes in direction
    • Pivot on one skate, while swinging the other around in an arc
  • Crossovers
    • Used to change direction while gaining speed
    • Step the outside foot over the inside, then push off with the inside foot.
  • Hockey Stop
    • Used for abrupt stops and directional changes
    • Turn your body to lean inwards, lean your outside foot on your inner edge, and vise versa for your inside foot

Recreational vs Hockey Skating

Ice hockey has a more aggressive style of skating compared to recreational skating. Heres a few obvious differences:

  • A hockey skater's stance is lower than a normal skater for more balance.
  • Ice Hockey forward strides are short and explosive for more speed.
  • Ice hockey players make more use of their blade edges for sharp cuts and agility.
